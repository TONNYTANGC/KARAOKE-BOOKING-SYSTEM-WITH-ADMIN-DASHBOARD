Welcome to Arcade Karaoke

Lets Move & Refresh Your Mind!

Our Room Book a Room

Step into a world of exhilarating fun and nostalgia at Arcade Game Vmall Karaoke!

Located at Vmall Northern University Malaysia, the "Arcade Game Vmall" is a vibrant and exciting entertainment shop that caters to the diverse interests of students and visitors alike.

  • An extensive collection of songs in multiple languages.
  • Private and stylishly designed karaoke rooms.
  • Using state-of-the-art audiovisual technology.

With that, we invite you to immerse yourself in an unforgettable karaoke experience that harmonizes pure entertainment with your passion for music. Whether belting out your favorite ballads with friends or showcasing your vocal prowess solo, prepare to be captivated by the euphoria of the spotlight and the resounding applause that awaits you at the Arcade Game Vmall's karaoke haven.
